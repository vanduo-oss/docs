Expanding Cards

A horizontal strip of panels that expand on focus — one stays wide while the others stay slim. Click a panel to expand it; use ← → when a panel is focused.

Class reference

Class / attributeDescription
.vd-expanding-cardsFlex row container. Initialized automatically unless data-vd-expanding-cards="manual".
.vd-expanding-cardSingle panel. Active panel has .is-active.
--vd-expanding-card-bgBackground image or color for the panel.
--vd-expanding-card-bg-defaultFallback color (nth-child presets or your own) — drives icon circle text color.
.vd-expanding-card-shadowBottom gradient scrim.
.vd-expanding-card-labelIcon + title row anchored to the corner.

JS: VanduoExpandingCards.init() runs with Vanduo.init(). Use Vanduo.reinit('expandingCards') after injecting markup.
