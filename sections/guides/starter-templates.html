Starter Landing Template Guide

This guide keeps one intentionally simple, comment-heavy landing page you can copy into a blank file. Advanced ready-made templates now live in the Templates catalog, where each template has its own notes and live preview.

Use this as your base pattern. Start with a navbar, hero, proof section, feature cards, CTA, and footer. Then graduate to the full templates when you need a more complete page.

Commented Landing Page

The comments explain why each block exists. Keep them while learning, then remove them once the page structure is familiar.
